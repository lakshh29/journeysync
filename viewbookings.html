FAQs

I am unable to go beyond the search option. What do I do?
If you are unable to go beyond the search option, please ensure your internet connection is stable and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact customer support.
How to book a bus ticket on JourneySync?
To book a ticket, log in to the JourneySync website or app, enter your travel details in the search bar, select a bus, choose your seat, and make the payment.
Where can I view the bus ticket that I have booked through JourneySync?
You can view your ticket under the 'My Bookings' section on the JourneySync app or website.
How to check the availability of buses?
Enter your travel route and date in the search bar to check available buses on JourneySync.
Do I need to create an account to book bus tickets on JourneySync?
No, you can book tickets as a guest, but creating an account lets you track bookings easily.
How to sign up or log in to JourneySync?
Click on the 'Sign Up' or 'Log In' button on the top right of the JourneySync homepage and follow the instructions.
Can I book State Road Transport Corporation bus tickets from JourneySync?
Yes, JourneySync allows you to book State Road Transport Corporation tickets. Search for your route to see available buses.
