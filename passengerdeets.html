Free Cancellation Policy on JourneySync 🚆

JourneySync is dedicated to providing a seamless and worry-free travel experience. Our Free Cancellation Policy ensures flexibility in your travel plans, with generous refund terms that stand out!

Benefits of Free Cancellation with JourneySync

  • Zero cancellation fee for cancellations made 36 hours before train departure.
  • 50% refund for cancellations made within 36 hours of train departure.
  • Instant processing of eligible refunds.
  • Dedicated customer support to address your queries promptly.

How It Works:

1️⃣ Opt for ‘Free Cancellation’ while booking your train ticket on the JourneySync app.

2️⃣ Cancel your ticket 36 hours before departure for a full refund of the ticket fare with no penalties.

3️⃣ For cancellations made within 36 hours of departure, enjoy a 50% refund.

4️⃣ Refunds will be processed instantly, ensuring a hassle-free experience.

Terms and Conditions:

The Free Cancellation service is valid only on train tickets booked via the JourneySync app.

Full refunds are applicable only for cancellations made 36 hours prior to the train departure.

For cancellations made within 36 hours of train departure, a 50% refund of the ticket fare will be processed.

Free Cancellation premiums, app convenience fees, and payment gateway charges (if any) are non-refundable.

This service is not valid for tickets where cancellation is attempted after the train departure.

Misuse or abuse of this service may result in cancellation of benefits and tickets without refund.

JourneySync reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate this policy at any time without prior notice.

All disputes are subject to the jurisdiction of New Delhi courts.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Free Cancellation?

Free Cancellation is a feature that allows you to cancel your train tickets without incurring penalties, ensuring you get a full or partial refund as per the cancellation timeline.

2. How can I avail Free Cancellation?

Simply opt for the Free Cancellation service during the booking process on the JourneySync app.

3. What are the benefits of Free Cancellation?

Full refund of the ticket fare for cancellations made 36 hours before departure.

50% refund for cancellations made within 36 hours of departure.

Instant refund processing for all eligible cancellations.

4. How many hours before the train departure can I cancel my ticket?

You can cancel your ticket up to the departure time. However:

Cancel before 36 hours: Receive a full refund.

Cancel within 36 hours: Receive a 50% refund.

5. How will I receive the refund if I opt for Free Cancellation?

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during booking.

6. How long does it take for the refund to reflect in my account?

Eligible refunds will be processed instantly; however, the actual time to reflect in your account may vary based on your bank or payment provider.

7. Will I get a full refund if I cancel my booking?

Yes, a full refund is provided if you cancel 36 hours prior to departure. Otherwise, you will receive a 50% refund.

8. What happens if the train gets cancelled?

If the train is cancelled by Indian Railways, JourneySync will provide a full refund regardless of the timeline.

9. When does the train chart get prepared?

The train chart is generally prepared 4-6 hours before departure, as per Indian Railways rules.
